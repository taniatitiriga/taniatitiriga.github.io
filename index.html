BSides Transylvania 2024

By ITania, Posted on 03/23/2024

I have had the amazing opportunity to be invited alongside my team (UVT-CTF) to Bsides Transylvania 2024, my first cybersecurity event. I dare to say this experience has given me confidence that I want to pursue a career in this field.

The conferences have inspired me so much; my favourite one has to be Jenny Radcliffe's, on the subject of social engineering. Her charisma and passion for this subject made her speech absolutely mesmerizing to watch.

Taking part in the CTF was another great experience. Our team got the 4th place and I'm glad to have scored points for us in a Cryptography challenge.
